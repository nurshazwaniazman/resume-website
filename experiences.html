JOB EXPERIENCES

LEADER OF DROPSHIP OF PLANNER BOOK (SEPTEMBER 2020 - APRIL 2021)

Work as a leader of dropship of planner book under Tarbiah Cinta Bookstore. It is a company that sells various of Islamic books, Quran, and planner books from Syabab since 2015.

Job duties :

  • Replying the dropship’s inquiries.
  • Updating the sales of dropships every month.
  • Informing the top dropships by month to the CEO.
  • Promoting the dropship intake.
  • Manage the intake of dropship.
  • Record all the dropship’s personal information.

ONLINE EDITOR (OCTOBER 2021 - FEBRUARY 2022)

Work as a online worker for image editing under Urbancolabb. It is an online shop that focuses on Muslimah fashion (hijab) and book planner since 2020, previously known as Mirasya HQ.

Job duties :

  • Editing the various marketing posters via Canva applications.
  • Alert with business information such as sale period, special events, announcements to be put inside the poster.
  • Consult with the CEO to get approval to use the poster.
  • Downloading the image and mailing it to the CEO and manager that handle the Urbancolabb’s social media accounts.

PART TIME CATERING (JUNE 2022)

Work as a part time catering at Setia Convention Centre (SCC) for event purposes that was organised by UiTM.

Job duties :

  • Do the preparation things before the events start including preparing and arranging tables and chairs for the guests.
  • Prepare the food and drink at the table.
  • Serving food and drink for the guests.
  • Help the guest if asked by them.
  • Clean the tables, and halls after the events end.
